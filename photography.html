Photography


Photography has been a passion of mine since I was about 11 years old, when I first took a picture on my dad's Nikon P80. Over the years I've dabbled in various forms of photography, from landscapes to portraits to wildlife photography (arguably my favorite), and have found it thrilling to strive towards getting just the right shot. ATL in the Clouds

One of the projects I have been working on since moving to Atlanta has been photographing the city's iconic skyline during moments where the clouds skim the tops of the buildings. This produces what is (in my opinion) a really beautiful effect with the buildings disappearing into the sky. It's a very loosely defined project, and something I try to add to whenever inspiration (or the weather) strikes.
