Welcome

Hello! I am a PhD student and Graduate Research Assistant at Georgia State University in Atlanta, Georgia. I started at GSU in Fall 2024, and I work with Dr. Doug Gies. My research is focused on studying massive binary stars in an effort to understand their evolution and what they can tell us about exotic astrophysical phenomena in the Universe. You can read about this here.

I attended Pennsylvania State University at University Park, Pennsylvania for my undergraduate studies. Here I received bachelor's of science degrees in Astronomy & Astrophysics, and Physics, as well as a minor in Mathematics.
